About

Wedson Motors Private Limited is a Start-up electric two-wheeler manufacturer integrating the development, production and sales of electric scooters and their accessories.

We are joining the India’s ongoing electric vehicle revolution to inspire a movement towards greener travel solutions.

Our vision is to be a part of India’s mobility transition and to achieve “An electric scooter for every Indian and keeping streets free of toxic vehicular exhaust.”
